Mission

About MonoMuse

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the evolving relationship between art, technology, and society. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by showcasing innovative exhibitions that highlight how digital tools, emerging technologies, and human imagination shape the future of artistic expression.

We believe everyone deserves access to the art of our time — and that art, at its best, helps us understand both the world and ourselves.

Our Journey

Milestones

  • 2016 MonoMuse opens its doors with its first exhibition on digital storytelling, welcoming over 3,000 visitors in the opening weekend.
  • 2018 Launch of the Interactive Media Lab, allowing visitors to experiment with creative coding and generative art tools.
  • 2020 Virtual programming launched during the pandemic, reaching 50,000+ online visitors across 30 countries.
  • 2022 MonoMuse awarded a national grant for digital arts education, funding new outreach programs for K-12 students.
  • 2023 Expansion of the museum space and launch of international artist collaborations with institutions in Berlin and Seoul.
  • 2026 Opening of the new Human × Machine gallery, our largest interactive installation space to date.
