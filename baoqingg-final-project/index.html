About the Museum

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the evolving relationship between art, technology, and society. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by showcasing innovative exhibitions that highlight how digital tools, emerging technologies, and human imagination shape the future of artistic expression.

Read More ↓

Since opening in 2016, MonoMuse has grown into a dynamic cultural institution that bridges the gap between traditional art appreciation and cutting-edge digital experiences. Our galleries rotate seasonal exhibitions featuring both established and emerging artists who work at the intersection of code, computation, and creativity. We believe that understanding technology is inseparable from understanding contemporary art — and that both should be accessible to everyone. Whether you are a student, a seasoned collector, or simply curious, MonoMuse offers something to surprise and inspire you.
