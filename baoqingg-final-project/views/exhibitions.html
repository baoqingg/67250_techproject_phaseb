On View Now

Exhibition 1: Digital Dreams — Art in the Age of Algorithms
Interactive · AI & Generative Art

This exhibition explores how artists are using algorithms, artificial intelligence, and generative tools to create new forms of digital expression. Through interactive displays and dynamic visuals, visitors can see how machines and human creativity work together to produce art that evolves in real time.

Dates: March 1 – June 30, 2026  |  Gallery: East Wing, Level 2

Exhibition 2: Code & Canvas — The Language of Creative Technology
Projections · Interactive Installations

Code & Canvas highlights the ways programming has become a medium for artistic creation. Featuring works by digital artists and designers, the exhibition showcases installations, projections, and interactive pieces that reveal how lines of code can transform into immersive visual experiences.

Dates: April 5 – August 15, 2026  |  Gallery: West Wing, Level 1

Exhibition 3: Synthetic Landscapes — AI-Generated Natural Worlds
Photography · AI Art · Environment

Synthetic Landscapes presents a collection of AI-generated photographic landscapes that blur the line between the real and the imagined. The works prompt visitors to reconsider notions of nature, authenticity, and environmental memory in a digitally saturated world.

Dates: May 10 – September 30, 2026  |  Gallery: Main Atrium

Exhibition 4: Human × Machine — Collaborative Creativity
Performance · Collaboration · Interactive

Human × Machine brings together human artists and AI systems in live and recorded collaborations. The exhibition challenges assumptions about authorship, creativity, and the role of the artist in a world where machines can paint, compose, and write.

Dates: June 1 – October 31, 2026  |  Gallery: Performance Space, Level 3
